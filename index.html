Wayne Gretzky

Age:

57

Hometown:

Bio:

Wayne Gretzky is called by many, the greatest hockey player ever. Gretzky's nickname is "The Great One".

Over his career he played for the Edmonton Oilers, LA Kings, St. Louis Blues, and the New York Rangers.

He shares or holds 61 records in the NHL.

A few notables are:

  • Most Goals: 894
  • Most Goals in one season: 92
  • Most Assists: 2,222
  • Most Assists in one season: 163
  • Most Points: 3,238
  • Most Points in one season: 215

Achievements:

  • 9 time NHL Most Valuable Player
  • 10 time Art Ross Trophy winner
  • 4 time Stanley Cup Champion
